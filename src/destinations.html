Destinations

Discover India's most enchanting destinations, each offering unique experiences that will create memories to last a lifetime.

Oct - Mar

Rajasthan

Land of kings and majestic palaces, where every fort tells a story of valor and grandeur. Experience royal heritage, desert safaris, and vibrant culture in cities like Jaipur, Udaipur, Jodhpur, and Jaisalmer.

Must Visit Places:

Amber Fort City Palace Thar Desert Hawa Mahal Lake Palace Mehrangarh Fort

Experiences:

Desert Safari Royal Palaces Cultural Shows Camel Rides
Nov - Feb

Goa

Tropical paradise with pristine beaches, Portuguese heritage, and vibrant nightlife. Perfect blend of relaxation and adventure with beautiful coastline and rich colonial history.

Famous Beaches:

Baga Beach Anjuna Beach Calangute Beach Palolem Beach

Activities:

Water Sports Nightlife Portuguese Architecture Beach Parties
Sep - Mar

Kerala

God's Own Country with serene backwaters, lush hill stations, and rich Ayurvedic traditions. Nature's masterpiece with tea gardens, spice plantations, and houseboat cruises.

Top Attractions:

Alleppey Backwaters Munnar Hills Cochin Thekkady

Experiences:

Houseboat Cruise Ayurveda Treatments Spice Gardens Kathakali Shows
Oct - Mar

Agra

Home to the eternal symbol of love, the Taj Mahal. Marvel at Mughal architecture and immerse in historical grandeur with magnificent monuments and rich history.

Historical Sites:

Taj Mahal Agra Fort Fatehpur Sikri Itimad-ud-Daulah

Specialties:

Mughal Architecture Marble Inlay Work Petha Sweets Leather Goods
